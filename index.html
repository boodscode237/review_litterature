2018
Full Paper

Main Results

GeoTxt outperforms the GeoNames web service by 20% in toponym resolution accuracy. The use of hierarchical disambiguation and proximity-based methods improves the system's accuracy, achieving 87.49% accuracy with CogComp NER.

2017
Full Paper

Main Results

TAGGS improved the accuracy of geoparsing by grouping tweets mentioning the same toponym. It achieved a recall of 0.82 and a precision of 0.91, significantly outperforming individual geoparsing methods.
